Buy or sell your real estate with just a click.

The Mortgage Consortium app enables a fully digital and direct transfer of real-estate properties with no personal meeting required. Buying or selling a property has never been easier.

Buy the Property On-line

Compare and pick the best mortgage loan from the comfort of your home. Or use Ethereum to buy instantly. No visits to the bank required.

Sell Quickly to Trustworthy Buyers

Sell your real-estate property to a buyer with a verified credit score. Sign the purchase contract on-line and save the costs of additional services.

Trust and Transparency

Your personal data are stored on your device and only you control who and when can access them. All data transactions are backed by Blockchain technology, which minimizes the risk of fraud.

Sell directly to save time and costs

Deal with trustworthy buyers. All prospecive buyers in the Mortgage Consortium app have passed a credit score test, which minimizes the sale risk.

Enjoy a transparent process. You will get a notification when the buyer's mortgage application is approved. Save phone calls by checking the current status in your mobile app.

Generate and sign the purchase contract on-line. The purchase contract generated by the Mortgage Consortium is fair for both parties and approved by banks which makes the mortgage financing process as smooth as possible.

Forget the paperwork. Just use your mobile phone.

Use your mobile phone camera to take pictures of your real estate property and answer a few questions about the important details that byuers and banks need to know. That's a big time saver as you don't need to meet with the bank's real estate evaluator.

Buy a real estate property you can afford

Compare and pick the best mortgage loan. Just answer a few necessary questions about you and your home to see a personalised offering of mortgage loans to choose from. Save time by avoiding visits to all the mortgage banks operating on the market today. It's as transparent as it can be.

What you see is what you pay. The price of the real estate property that you see has already been evaluated by the bank, which avoids any nasty surprises. The price tag contains all the costs and there are no hidden fees.

Connect with your mortgage specialist remotely. Although the app guides you through the whole buying process, you can reach to your mortgage specialist any time via chat or audio/video call.
