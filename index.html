Next generation of access to housing.

This project aims to test new technologies and verify their applicability in the real world. All interested parties are welcome.

Ethereum

You can buy or sell your house or flat via Ethereum Blockchain. Our platform generate your wallet and manage your assets for the simplicity.

Consortium

Build a consortium on Azure Confidential Ledger that runs exclusively on the trusted execution environments. ACL provides decentralized ledger for data transactions backed by Blockchain. Use many resources of Azure to store, manage and integrate off-chain data.

Verifiable Credentials

This feature will help you to expose and prove only credentials that are required by the steps in the process of making a purchase or sale. You can decide what to expose to whom and when.

Joining us is a pleasure

Our platform provides the process of buying/selling property in the role you prefer (Buyer or Seller). Only the Bank mortgage specialist is a simulated role. Also we are working on the possibility to share the ownership between multiple applicants. Would you like to dry run the process? Let us know! We have to evaluate your requests cause we are not able to cover all of them.

Just like a game. Start the journey on the preferred platform (mobile,web). Invite your friend or family to be a participant and go through the process step by step. It is up to you how far you can get. Currently we have created an android application for the end to end process. (web will be available soon)

Real Estate Services

This platform offers two ways how to buy or sell your property. Direct way via ETH or via fully digital mortgage process that is built on top of the verified flow. You could use our APIs to integrate with your current solution.
